IoT based Health Monitoring Kit

Nov 2018

This is a mini project of 6th semester of ECE as per the syllabus of TOE. I collaborated with a group of 4 students from same faculty to develop a prototype of a affordable and portable health kit. This prototype could measure the body temperature and pulse-rate of a patient and display the same in webpage. Also, Electrocardiograph(ECG) of patient was displayed using python graph.

This prototype used Arduino as microcontroller and SIM800A GSM/GPRS module was used to send the value of measured body parameters to the webpage.

During the project, I developed the webpage, configured and programmed ECG graph and GSM/GPRS module.
